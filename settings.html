◆ Intent

Configure your LLM backend

  1. Select any text on a webpage, then press Ctrl+Shift+S (Mac: ⌘⇧S) — Intent classifies it and saves a card to your feed.
  2. No text selected? Press the shortcut anyway — Intent saves the page title and URL as an article capture.
  3. Type a raw thought directly in the extension popup and click Save.
  4. Click the ◆ icon to browse your feed, filter by intent type, or export.

Runs entirely on-device — no API key required. Requires Chrome 127+ with Built-in AI enabled (chrome://flags/#optimization-guide-on-device-model). If unavailable, Intent automatically falls back to Gemini Cloud when a key is configured below.
