Intent — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What Intent collects

Intent saves the following data locally on your device when you capture something:

  • The text you selected (or the page title and URL if nothing was selected)
  • The URL and title of the page you were on
  • An AI-generated intent classification, title, and one-line reason
  • A timestamp

Where your data is stored

All captures are stored in chrome.storage.local on your device. Your settings (API provider and key) are stored in chrome.storage.sync, which is encrypted and tied to your Google account.

If you configure Supabase sync, your captures are additionally stored in the Supabase project you own and control. Intent never sends your data to any server operated by the extension developer.

Third-party services

Intent contacts the following external services only when you explicitly configure them:

  • Anthropic API — if you select Anthropic as your LLM provider. Your captured text is sent to Anthropic to perform classification. Anthropic's privacy policy applies.
  • LM Studio (localhost) — a local inference server you run on your own machine. No data leaves your device.
  • Supabase — a database service you provision and control. Data is sent only to your own Supabase project using credentials you provide.

What Intent does not do

  • Does not collect analytics or telemetry
  • Does not share your data with the extension developer or any third party
  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not read page content beyond what you explicitly capture

Permissions

Intent requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save and retrieve your captures and settings
  • activeTab — to read the URL and title of the current tab when you trigger a capture
  • scripting — to read your text selection from the active tab
  • tabs — to access the active tab's metadata

Data deletion

You can delete individual captures from the extension popup. To delete all data, remove the extension from Chrome — this clears chrome.storage.local automatically. If you enabled Supabase sync, delete records directly from your Supabase project.
